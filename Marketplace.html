How It Works

ORYN Marketplace is exclusive to campus students — buy and sell safely within your community

1

Post Your Item

List anything you want to sell — add a description, price, condition, and your student ID.

2

Buyers Contact You

Interested students send you a message through the campus messaging system directly.

3

Meet on Campus

Arrange a safe handover on campus — Library, Cafe, or the Student Services block.

4

Complete the Sale

Exchange the item and payment in person. Rate the experience to build trust in the community.
